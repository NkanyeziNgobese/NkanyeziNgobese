## About Me

I am a Computer Science Honours graduate and Graduate Test Engineer based in South Africa. My work sits at the intersection of software quality, applied AI, data-driven systems, learning technology, and finance technology.

I enjoy building systems that turn complex ideas into usable tools, especially where software engineering, testing, data, and education meet.

## Current Focus

* **Software Testing & Quality Engineering** - app testing, API testing, regression testing, test environment maintenance, and Java-based automation exposure.
* **AI, NLP & Learning Systems** - adaptive learning, sequence modeling, local-language AI, and knowledge-focused tooling.
* **Data & Systems Thinking** - simulation, data wrangling, dashboards, and practical decision-support projects.
* **Finance + Technology** - investment literacy, risk thinking, banking technology, and financial education tools.

## Featured Work

* **FinancialQuest** - an Honours research project exploring a Unity-based, ontology-aligned adaptive learning prototype for investment literacy education in South Africa.
* **Durban Port Congestion Simulation** - a data and systems simulation project focused on modelling congestion patterns and operational bottlenecks.
* **AlgoRecall** - a private learning system for practicing data structures, algorithms, concurrency, and system design through recall-based practice.
* **IsiZulu -> English Translator** - an NLP project exploring local-language translation and linguistic accessibility.
* **RSA Coin Recognition** - an image-processing project focused on recognising South African coins.

## Collaboration Interests

I am open to collaborating on projects involving:

* Software testing and quality engineering
* AI / machine learning
* NLP and local-language technology
* Gamified learning and education technology
* Data-driven simulations and decision-support systems
* Finance technology and investment-literacy tools
